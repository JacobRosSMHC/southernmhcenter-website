Every Situation Covered

We Do All Types of Loans

We don't just work with one lender or one loan type — we shop your application across all our lending partners to find the best fit for your situation.

🏠

Chattel Loans

The most common loan for manufactured homes. The home itself is the collateral — great if you're placing on leased land or a family lot. Fast approval, competitive rates.

🏡

Land-Home Loans

Finance your home and land together in one loan. Ideal if you're buying land or already own it. Typically lower rates and longer terms than chattel.

🏛️

FHA Loans

Government-backed loan with as little as 3.5% down. Easier credit requirements make this a great option for first-time buyers or those rebuilding credit.

🎖️

VA Loans

Exclusively for veterans, active-duty service members, and surviving spouses. Often $0 down with no PMI. We are proud to serve those who served.

🌾

USDA / Rural Development

$0 down financing for eligible rural and suburban buyers through USDA's Rural Development program. Much of Louisiana qualifies — ask us if your address is eligible.

📋

Conventional Loans

Traditional financing for buyers with strong credit and solid down payment. Can offer the best long-term rates when the home is titled as real property.

Not sure which loan type fits your situation? Fill out the application above and we'll figure it out together.
