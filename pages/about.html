About Southern MHC

We got tired of watching people get treated like transactions.

So we built something different. A team of regular people from completely different walks of life — united by one belief: that buying a home should feel like a milestone, not a trap.

When you trust us to guide you through one of the biggest purchases of your life, that's not a commission to us. That's a responsibility.

Close to 40 years of combined experience. Not one of us came from the same place.

Sales floors. Mechanic bays. Manufacturing plants. Restaurant kitchens. We're not the stereotypical "sales team." We're a group of people who grew up working, who know what it means to stretch a dollar, and who ended up in this industry for one reason or another — and eventually had enough of watching it be done the wrong way.

Here's what we kept seeing at other places: the customer gets treated great right up until the check clears. After that? Good luck getting a callback. Issues get minimized. Promises made during the sale quietly disappear. The people who put their trust — and their life savings — into a home get handed off like they don't matter anymore.

We all hit the same wall at different times, at different places. And at some point, you either accept it, or you decide to do something about it.

We decided to do something about it.

Southern Mobile Home Center exists because we believe this industry can treat people better. That it should. We're not here to move units. We're here to help families get into homes the right way — transparently, honestly, and with someone who actually picks up the phone when you call.

What We Walked Away From

The old playbook at the dealerships we came from.

  • ✗ Customers treated great until the deal closes — then ignored
  • ✗ Promises made during the sale that quietly disappear afterward
  • ✗ Pricing designed to confuse, not inform
  • ✗ Buyers pushed toward whatever earns the most commission
  • ✗ No one picking up the phone post-delivery
  • ✗ Treating every customer like a number on a board
The Southern MHC Way

What we built instead.

  • ✓ The relationship doesn't end at closing — we stay accountable
  • ✓ Everything confirmed in writing, every time
  • ✓ Pricing that's transparent and explained clearly
  • ✓ We find the right home for you — not the most convenient one for us
  • ✓ Direct team contact at every step of the process
  • ✓ Every customer is a person trusting us with a major life decision

Our Commitment

What we promise every single customer.

These aren't marketing lines. They're the standards we hold ourselves to — and the ones we'd want if we were in your shoes.

01

Transparency from the first conversation.

You'll know what things cost, what's included, and what's not — before you ever sign anything. No bait-and-switch. No fine print surprises.

02

We put your needs above an easy sale.

If a home doesn't fit your life, we'll tell you. We'd rather lose a deal than send someone into the wrong situation. Our reputation is built on people who are genuinely happy long after move-in day.

03

We handle the hard parts — in-house.

Delivery, setup, financing coordination — we manage it ourselves. No handoffs to strangers. One team, start to finish, who knows your name and your situation.

04

We're here after the sale. Full stop.

You'll have a direct line to real people on this team — not a corporate 800 number. If something comes up, call us. That's not a slogan. That's the deal.

No hidden add-ons. Ever.

Every price we show already includes all of this. What you see is what you actually get.
