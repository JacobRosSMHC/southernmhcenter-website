How to Use This Tool

1
Pick a model from the dropdown. A gold rectangle will appear showing its exact footprint.

Enter your address and click Search. The satellite map zooms to your property.
3
Drag the rectangle to position the home anywhere on your lot.
4
Rotate in 15° increments using the buttons above the map to try different orientations.
5
Happy with the fit? Click "Request a Quote" and our team will reach out within one business day.
